introducing my favourite projects and experiences...

👋 Welcome to the page where I showcase all of the things I love to work on. Right now, I'm focussing on health & technology, social impact, and community service, specifically working towards addressing UN SDG #3 (Good Health & Well-Being) and UN SDG #10 (Reduced Inequalities). I'm also working on challenging myself and developing skills in a variety of different areas, whether that be research, software development, public speaking, or project management. Interested in collaborating? Shoot me an email at jennifer.tsai@uwaterloo.ca!
